About Us

Rina Gupteshwar Food - Bihar Bhojpur Shahpur

Welcome to Rina Gupteshwar Food, where we serve authentic Bihari cuisine with love and tradition. Located in the heart of Shahpur, Bhojpur, we take pride in preserving the rich culinary heritage of Bihar.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring that every dish we serve carries the authentic flavors of traditional Bihari home cooking.

We use locally sourced ingredients and traditional cooking methods to bring you the true taste of Bihar. From our signature Litti Chokha to our delicious sweets, every item on our menu tells a story of Bihar's culinary culture.
